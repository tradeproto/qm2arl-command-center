## 1. Purpose

Establish controls for design activities affecting nuclear safety-related items.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all Chapman Nuclear design outputs: calculations, drawings, specifications, and software used in design.

## 3. Design Input

Inputs are obtained from customer specifications, applicable codes (ASME, ANSI), and regulatory requirements. Inputs are reviewed by the lead engineer and filed in the project folder.

## 4. Design Output

Outputs include SolidWorks models, fabrication drawings, and stress analysis reports. PE review is required before release to manufacturing.

## 5. Design Verification

Verification methods:
- Design review meetings (primary method)
- Hand calculations cross-check (when time permits)
- FEA validation against prior test data (select projects)

**Note:** Verification is not always performed by personnel independent of the design originator due to staffing constraints.

## 6. Design Changes

Changes after release require ECO form ENG-ECO-01. Customer approval obtained for safety-related changes.

## 7. Computer Software

SolidWorks and ANSYS are approved for structural design. **QM2ARL optimization outputs are used experimentally** — not yet in the approved software list or qualified per IEEE 1012 / NQA-1 software verification requirements.
