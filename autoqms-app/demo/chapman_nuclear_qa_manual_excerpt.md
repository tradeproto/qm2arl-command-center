## 1. Organization (NQA-1 Req. 1)

Chapman Nuclear LLC maintains a Quality Assurance Organization reporting to the President. The QA Manager has authority to stop work affecting quality or safety. Organization chart is maintained in HR records but is not posted in the controlled document system.

## 2. Quality Assurance Program (NQA-1 Req. 2)

The QA program applies to design, procurement, fabrication, and field services for nuclear components. A top-level QA manual exists. Sub-tier suppliers are required to maintain compatible QA programs. **Gap note:** AI-assisted design tools are used in SolidWorks workflows but are not yet listed in the approved software register.

## 3. Design Control (NQA-1 Req. 3)

Design activities are controlled through ENG-DC-100. Design inputs are documented on customer POs and specification sheets. Independent design verification is performed by a second engineer when schedule permits. **Gap note:** 3D-printed part qualification focuses on final part inspection; code qualification for additive manufacturing build files is not yet formalized.

## 4. Document Control (NQA-1 Req. 6)

Procedures and drawings are controlled in a shared network drive. Revision history is tracked in filenames (e.g., `_RevB`). Master list of controlled documents exists as a spreadsheet updated quarterly. **Gap note:** No cryptographic hash or blockchain attestation on signed policy documents.

## 5. Inspection & Test (NQA-1 Req. 10–11)

Inspection plans reference customer specifications. Test results are recorded in traveler packages. Hold points are defined for critical welds. **Gap note:** Digital traveler packages are not uniformly indexed for audit retrieval.

## 6. Nonconforming Items & CAPA (NQA-1 Req. 15–16)

NCRs are logged in a paper logbook. Corrective actions are assigned verbally in production meetings. **Gap note:** CAPA closure evidence is inconsistent; no formal link between audit findings and CAPA tracking system.

## 7. Audits (NQA-1 Req. 18)

Internal audits are conducted annually by the QA Manager. Last internal audit: 2023-09. **Gap note:** Internal auditor independence is not documented when QA Manager performs the audit.
