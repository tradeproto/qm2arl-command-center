## 1. Purpose

Qualify suppliers of items and services affecting nuclear quality.

## 2. Initial Qualification

New suppliers complete a questionnaire (FORM-SUP-01) and provide:
- Quality program description
- NQA-1 or ISO 9001 certificate (if applicable)
- Reference customer list

QA Manager reviews and approves. Critical suppliers require an on-site audit within 24 months.

## 3. Surveillance

Approved supplier list maintained in Excel. Re-evaluation every 3 years or upon significant nonconformance.

## 4. Gaps Identified (Self-Assessment 2025)

- Supplier audit reports from 2021–2023 are in paper files, not digitized
- No automated tracking of supplier certificate expiration dates
- Sub-tier supplier flow-down requirements referenced verbally, not always in PO language
- **No AI-assisted review of supplier QA manuals** against NQA-1 requirements
