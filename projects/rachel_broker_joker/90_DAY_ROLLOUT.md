## Days 1–30 — Internal Installation

**Goal:** Install discipline into muscle memory. **Who:** Rachel + core team only.

### Week 1 — Authority Reset

| Day | Focus | Success signal | Done |
|-----|-------|----------------|------|
| 1 | Declaration + deploy tools + one enforcement act | Call ended early OR deal killed OR someone said less | ☐ |
| 2 | Observe — who adapts, who resists | Notes only, no coaching | ☐ |
| 3 | Observe — pattern recognition | Identify 1 potential enforcer | ☐ |
| 4 | Interrupt explaining when it happens | Team stops improvising language | ☐ |
| 5 | Review week — one sentence debrief to team | At least one uncomfortable moment logged | ☐ |

**Week 1 tools:** [`protocol/DESK_CARD.md`](protocol/DESK_CARD.md) · [`protocol/ONE_PAGE_SOP.md`](protocol/ONE_PAGE_SOP.md) · [`protocol/WHATSAPP_QUICK_GUIDE.md`](protocol/WHATSAPP_QUICK_GUIDE.md)

## Days 61–90 — Quiet Scale Activation

**Goal:** System works without promotion.

### Weeks 9–10 — Referral Test

Certified operators may **reference** the protocol. They may **not** explain or pitch it.

**Success metric:** Inbound questions arrive already respecting structure.

| Day | Done |
|-----|------|
| 61–74 | ☐ |

### Week 11 — Enterprise Signal

Quietly introduce team licenses and internal certification authority — **only when asked**.

**Success metric:** One organization asks about internal rollout.

| Day | Done |
|-----|------|
| 75–81 | ☐ |

### Week 12 — Rachel Steps Back

Remove Rachel from: discovery · early advisory · protocol explanation.

Rachel engages only when: certification exists · authority is present · stakes justify it.

**Success metric:** Calendar lighter, deal quality higher.

| Day | Done |
|-----|------|
| 82–90 | ☐ |
