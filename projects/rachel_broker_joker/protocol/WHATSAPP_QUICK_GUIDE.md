# WhatsApp Quick Guide — Joker Broker Protocol™

**INTERNAL ONLY — Core team WhatsApp / Signal group**

Pin this message. Do not debate it in chat.

---

## Group Rules

1. **No free consulting in chat.** Questions get one of the default lines below — not answers.
2. **No forwarding Rachel deal threads** without discovery facts filled in (template below).
3. **No “quick opinion”** on counterparties, structures, or timing. That is advisory.
4. **Silence is allowed.** Not every message needs a reply.

---

## Default Replies (Copy-Paste)

**Drift / scope creep:**
> That’s not something we cover at this stage.

**Free advice request:**
> Advisory is paid and deliberate. We can scope that separately.

**“Can Rachel look at this?”**
> Send discovery facts first. If it clears structure, we’ll scope advisory.

**Weak / incomplete deal:**
> This doesn’t meet our threshold.

**Protocol question from outsider:**
> The protocol is invitation-only.

---

## Discovery Fact Template

Before escalating to Rachel, paste this filled in:

```
Product/grade:
Volume/cadence:
Corridor (origin → destination):
Counterparty + role:
Timeline:
Incoterms (if known):
Ask (one sentence):
Source/referral:
```

If any field is blank → **do not escalate**.

---

## When to @ Rachel

- Enterprise license discussion (after they ask)
- Certified operator referral with cert ID confirmed
- Kill decision on high-stakes deal (post-facts, one paragraph max)
- Exception request (rare — she decides, team does not lobby)

## When NOT to @ Rachel

- “What do you think of this counterparty?”
- “Is this a good deal?”
- “Can you explain how we work?”
- Any thread that would require teaching the protocol
