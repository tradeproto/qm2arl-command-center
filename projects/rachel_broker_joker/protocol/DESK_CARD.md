# Desk Card — Joker Broker Protocol™

**INTERNAL ONLY — Do not distribute externally.**  
Print one per desk. Laminate if possible.

---

## The Standard (One Sentence)

**Access to judgment requires structure.**

---

## Three Modes

| Mode | What it is | What you do |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **Discovery** | Facts only | Ask. Listen. Time-box 15 min. Stop. |
| **Advisory** | Paid and deliberate | Scope agreed before depth. No free consulting. |
| **Protocol** | Certification required | No protocol pass → no access to Rachel or senior judgment |

---

## If the Conversation Drifts

Say only:

> **“That’s not something we cover at this stage.”**

Then **silence**. Do not explain. Do not rescue.

---

## Kill Rules (Non-Negotiable)

End the call or kill the deal when:

- Counterparty cannot name facts (volumes, ports, incoterms, timeline)
- Structure is unclear after 15 minutes
- Someone asks “how does this work” before qualification
- Helpfulness would replace structure
- Rachel would need to explain the standard to proceed

---

## Default Lines

| Situation | Line |
|-----------|------|
| Scope creep | “Advisory is paid and deliberate. We can scope that separately.” |
| Free consulting request | “We don’t provide judgment without structure.” |
| Pressure to explain protocol | “The protocol is invitation-only. This isn’t that stage.” |
| Weak deal | “This doesn’t meet our threshold. We’re done here.” |
