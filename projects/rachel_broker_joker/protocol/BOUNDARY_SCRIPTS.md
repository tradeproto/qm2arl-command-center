# Boundary Scripts — Joker Broker Protocol™

**INTERNAL ONLY**

Use verbatim. Do not soften. Do not add Q&A after the declaration.

---

## Day 1 Declaration (Team — Live or Written)

> From today forward, access to judgment requires structure.  
> Discovery is facts only.  
> Advisory is paid and deliberate.  
> If a conversation drifts, we end it.

**Stop talking.** No Q&A. No debate. Silence after this is intentional.

---

## Deployment Line (When Handing Out Tools)

> These replace improvisation.

Nothing else.

---

## Drift Line (Default)

> That’s not something we cover at this stage.

Then silence.

---

## Discovery Open

> This is discovery — facts only. We have fifteen minutes.

---

## Discovery Close — Proceed

> We have enough to scope advisory. You’ll receive a scope document. No deep work until that’s agreed.

---

## Discovery Close — Kill

> This doesn’t meet our threshold. We’re done here.

Hang up or leave the room. No cushion.

---

## Free Consulting Redirect

> We don’t provide judgment without structure. If you want advisory, we scope it separately.

---

## “How Does This Work?” (Pre-Qualification)

> The protocol is invitation-only. This isn’t that stage.

Do not explain further.

---

## Scope Creep on Active Call

> That’s advisory scope. We can address it under a separate engagement.

---

## Friend / Exception Request

> No exceptions. The standard applies to everyone.

---

## Certified Operator Referral (Inbound)

> Who referred you, and what is their certification status?

Verify before proceeding.

---

## Post-Kill (If They Push Back)

> The decision stands.

No relitigation.

---

## Rachel — Personal Enforcement Reminders

When you feel the urge to help, explain, or rescue:

1. Say the drift line  
2. Count to ten in silence  
3. End the call if they continue  

Culture locks the first time it’s tested — especially when it costs you an opportunity.
