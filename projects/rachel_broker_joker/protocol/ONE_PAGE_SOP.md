# One-Page SOP — Joker Broker Protocol™

**INTERNAL ONLY**  
Version 0.1 · 2026-06-27

---

## Purpose

Replace improvisation with a repeatable standard for discovery, advisory, and access to judgment on global commodity and trade intelligence engagements.

---

## Scope

Applies to: Rachel, core team, and all future certified operators.  
Does not apply to: public book readers, uninvited inbound, or uncertified counterparties seeking free consulting.

---

## Definitions

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Discovery** | Fact-gathering only. No advice. No structure recommendations. Max 15 minutes unless extended in writing. |
| **Advisory** | Paid engagement with defined scope, deliverable, and timeline. |
| **Protocol** | Internal certification standard. Required before senior judgment, deal validation, or Rachel’s direct involvement. |
| **Kill** | End call or decline deal. Required when standards are not met. |

---

## Process

### 1. Inbound contact

1. Classify: discovery · advisory inquiry · referral · noise  
2. If discovery → schedule 15-minute call, send no prep materials  
3. If advisory → send scope questionnaire; no call until scope acknowledged  
4. If referral from certified operator → verify certification before proceeding  
5. If noise → decline without explanation  

### 2. Discovery call (15 minutes)

1. Open: “This is discovery — facts only.”  
2. Required facts (commodity context — customize):
   - Product / grade
   - Volume and cadence
   - Origin / destination / corridor
   - Counterparty role (who holds what risk)
   - Timeline and incoterms (if known)
   - What they want from Rachel (one sentence)
3. At 15 minutes → hard stop regardless of momentum  
4. Outcomes: **Proceed to advisory scope** · **Kill** · **Refer to certified operator**

### 3. Advisory engagement

1. Written scope signed before deep work  
2. Retainer or fixed fee — no hourly improvisation  
3. Deliverable named (memo, briefing, validation, go/no-go)  
4. No scope creep without written change order  

### 4. Protocol gate (before Rachel)

Candidate or counterparty must have:

- [ ] Completed certification (internal or external track), OR  
- [ ] Enterprise license with internal cert authority, OR  
- [ ] Rachel’s explicit exception (rare — logged)

No exception for friends. No “just this once.”

---

## Enforcement

| Violation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Explaining instead of enforcing | Interrupt publicly; reset call |
| Rescue after boundary line | Reset call; note for certification review |
| Free consulting | Redirect to paid scope or end |
| Drift past 15 min without extension | End call |

Rachel (or designated enforcer) must perform **at least one visible enforcement act per week** during installation phase.
